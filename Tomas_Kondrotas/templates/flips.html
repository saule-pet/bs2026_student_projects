Buy at — the lowest price someone recently sold for. That's what you offer when buying.
Sell at — the highest price someone recently paid. That's what you list it for when selling.
Margin — the difference between buy and sell. If you buy for 1k and sell for 1.5k, your margin is 500gp (50%).
Max profit — margin × your buy limit. The most you can make in one full flip cycle before the 4h GE cooldown resets.
1h volume — how many of this item traded in the last hour. Low volume = you might get stuck waiting for your offer to fill.

Items are ranked by a score that rewards high margin, high volume, and high max profit together — a great margin on something nobody trades is ranked lower than a decent margin on something that moves thousands per hour. Always verify prices in-game before committing — GE prices move fast.
